Satellite Reception

Summer 2023


Once again, we find ourselves delving into the realm of antenna design, this time focusing on a crafted patch  antenna optimized for 1.7 GHz frequencies. Regrettably, the simulations conducted prior to fabrication failed to fully align with the outcomes observed in the production , particularly in the case of two out of the three boards manufactured. It seems that the design's efficacy was too dependant on some lenghts, a factor that posed significant challenges during the production process. Note taked for the future.

These were made with a very specific process and a special type of board, the so called Rogers substrates.

Voice chess,
a Chess Board With Voice Commands


For all people with incapabilities (or just for the lazy guys), this board allows to play chess without hands. Using google´s funcionality that translates the voice to text, the commands of the movement are sent for the controller, that moves the pieces (and even remove them when they are killed) using a magnet. For more informations about the building, code, etc, check the Instructables page of the project. It was covered on medium.com, hackaday and tons of others websites!
